<p><p>The industrial heat transfer oil market can be classified into three main types: mineral oil, silicones and aromatics, and ethylene glycol. Silicones and aromatics are synthetic oils known for their excellent heat resistance and oxidation stability. Ethylene glycol, on the other hand, is a popular heat transfer fluid for low-temperature applications, commonly used in refrigeration systems. These different types of heat transfer oils cater to various industrial needs based on their specific properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
