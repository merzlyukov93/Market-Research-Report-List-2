<p><p>The Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) market can be categorized into two types: Modular IGBT and Discrete IGBT. </p><p>Modular IGBTs are integrated with other components, such as driver circuits and protection features, into a single module. They offer high power density and are commonly used in applications such as motor drives, renewable energy systems, and industrial automation.</p><p>On the other hand, Discrete IGBTs are standalone components that are not integrated into a module. They provide flexibility in design and are often used in applications where precise control and high performance are required, like in automotive systems, consumer electronics, and residential appliances.</p></p>
