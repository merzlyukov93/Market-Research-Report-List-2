<p><p>Tone generators are devices that produce various sound frequencies for different applications. They are commonly classified into low, warble, and high tones based on their frequency range. Low tones typically have frequencies below 200 Hz and are often used in music and multimedia production. Warble tones are wide-bandwidth signals that oscillate within a specific frequency range. They are commonly employed in testing equipment and as warning signals. On the other hand, high tones have frequencies above 1000 Hz and are often utilized in ultrasonic testing, scientific research, and sound therapy.</p></p>
