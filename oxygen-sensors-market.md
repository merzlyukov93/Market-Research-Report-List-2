<p><strong>The Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensor</li><li>Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant Potential Electrolysis Sensor is a type of oxygen sensor used to measure the oxygen concentration in various environments, such as in industrial processes and automotive applications. It operates by passing a constant current through an electrolyte, which generates oxygen ions. The concentration of oxygen is then determined by measuring the voltage across the sensor. </p><p>Galvanic Cell Type Gas Sensor, on the other hand, is a portable oxygen sensor commonly used for personal safety and industrial monitoring. It consists of two electrodes separated by an electrolyte, creating a chemical reaction that generates an electric current proportional to the oxygen concentration. The current is measured and converted into an output signal, providing real-time oxygen level readings.</p></p>
