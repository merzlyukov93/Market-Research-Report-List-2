<p><p>IGZO Display market includes two main types: Amorphous IGZO and Crystalline IGZO. Amorphous IGZO refers to the use of Indium-Gallium-Zinc-Oxide in a non-crystalline state, offering advantages like low power consumption and high transparency, suitable for mobile devices and high-resolution displays. On the other hand, Crystalline IGZO involves the use of Indium-Gallium-Zinc-Oxide in a crystalline state, offering improved electrical performance and better stability, making it ideal for larger displays like TVs and monitors. Both types cater to different market needs and provide varying benefits based on specific applications.</p></p>
